# Big Cats of the World Education Guide

> **Public collection demonstration only.** No payment processing. No checkout. No customer data collection. Only RC17-approved non-restricted assets are displayed.


## Learning goals

1. Distinguish species recognition from species understanding.
2. Explain why rights and attribution matter before publishing wildlife imagery.
3. Compare Lion and Tiger as public demo metadata assets.
4. Practice reading source notes and license information.

## Activity: Rights-aware species card

Ask learners to create a species card with four fields: common name, source authority, rights status, and attribution requirement. Learners should explain why an image cannot be assumed publishable just because metadata is approved.

## Discussion prompts

- What is the difference between a source record and an image license?
- Why are rights-restricted or unknown-rights assets excluded from this public release?
- How does attribution build trust in a public collection?
