# Big Cats of the World - Educational Notes

## Learning goals

- Identify major big cat species and habitats.
- Explain why apex predators can indicate ecosystem health.
- Compare grassland, forest, mountain, and rainforest habitats.
- Discuss conservation, coexistence, and habitat corridors.
- Interpret wildlife imagery responsibly.

## Vocabulary

- Apex predator
- Habitat corridor
- Biodiversity
- Conservation
- Camouflage
- Keystone species
- Human-wildlife coexistence

## Classroom prompts

1. What does a lion need that a poster cannot show?
2. Why might a tiger corridor matter more than a single protected place?
3. How can visual admiration support or distort conservation understanding?
4. What rights and responsibilities come with using wildlife images?

## Sensitivity and rights notes

- Wildlife photographs require rights-cleared imagery.
- Cultural references to big cats should be contextual and respectful.
- Conservation claims should be reviewed before publication.
