## H1: Oceans of the World

### Hero

Life and seascape from the sunlit reef to the abyss. The ocean covers most of our planet and remains its least-known frontier.

### Featured in this collection

Blue Whale (Balaenoptera musculus), Orca (Orcinus orca), Great White Shark (Carcharodon carcharias), Whale Shark (Rhincodon typus), Green Sea Turtle (Chelonia mydas), and more.

### Why this collection

The ocean is a single connected system, and this collection treats it as one: the blue whale, largest animal that has ever lived; the orca and great white at the top of the food web; the reef-building corals that quietly construct the most biodiverse habitats on Earth. It is also the realm most exposed to a changing climate — warming, acidifying, and bleaching — which makes its beauty an argument for its protection.

### Shop the collection

Strong decor and education category: canvas and framed marine prints, an oceans wall calendar, a reef puzzle, and educational card sets.
