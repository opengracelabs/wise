## Welcome

Thank you for joining us. Nature & Culture is building a permanent, freely accessible memory of the world's heritage, nature and culture — and bringing its stories to your wall, your shelf and your inbox.

## What to expect

Every month: a featured collection, a species in focus, new products, and the stories behind the objects — all backed by verified sources and open rights.

## Start exploring

Begin with three favourites: **Taj Mahal**, **Lion (Panthera leo)**, and **The Great Wave off Kanagawa**.

## A note on access

Canonical public memory is free, forever. Our shop simply lets you take a piece of it home.
