## H1: Sacred Mountains

### Hero

Peaks revered across faiths and cultures. Long before they were climbed, mountains were worshipped.

### Featured in this collection

Mount Fuji, Mount Everest, Mount Kilimanjaro, Uluru-Kata Tjuta, Mount Kenya, and more.

### Why this collection

From Fuji's near-perfect cone to the red monolith of Uluru, sacred mountains anchor the cosmologies of the peoples around them. Some are climbed in devotion, others left deliberately unclimbed out of respect. Together they show how landscape and belief shape one another — and why protecting a peak can mean protecting a culture.

### Shop the collection

Dramatic vertical compositions suit canvas and framed landscape prints, a mountains wall calendar, and a landscape coffee-table book.
