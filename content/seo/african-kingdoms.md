## H1: African Kingdoms

### Hero

The cities, courts and empires of Africa's past. Africa's history is a history of kingdoms — of gold and salt, of bronze and stone, of scholarship and faith.

### Featured in this collection

Great Zimbabwe, Benin Bronzes, Timbuktu, Pyramids of Meroe, Rock-Hewn Churches of Lalibela, and more.

### Why this collection

These are civilizations of the first rank: the brass casters of Benin, whose plaques rival any Renaissance relief; the Nubian pharaohs whose steep pyramids crowd the desert at Meroë; the Ethiopian masons who carved churches downward into living rock at Lalibela. Presenting them together is both an educational correction and a celebration — African achievement on its own terms.

### Shop the collection

A priority for representation and education: framed prints, an *African Kingdoms* coffee-table book, educational card sets, and culturally-attributed art reproductions.
