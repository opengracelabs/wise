## Introduction

Ancient Maps is a flagship draft collection for historic cartography and changing worldviews. It is designed for validation-only editorial, educational, and product exploration. The collection does not create canonical records, new agents, governance changes, or production commerce commitments.

## Educational narrative

This collection helps learners connect individual assets to wider systems of geography, culture, biodiversity, public memory, and stewardship. It is written for museum-shop discovery, classroom prompts, family learning, and future rights-cleared product testing.
