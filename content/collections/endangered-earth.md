# Endangered Earth

**Status:** RC14 flagship collection draft  
**Architecture boundary:** Implementation content only; Architecture v1.0 remains frozen under ADR-011.

## Introduction

Endangered Earth is a flagship draft collection for biodiversity, climate, and stewardship education. It is designed for validation-only editorial, educational, and product exploration. The collection does not create canonical records, new agents, governance changes, or production commerce commitments.

## Educational narrative

This collection helps learners connect individual assets to wider systems of geography, culture, biodiversity, public memory, and stewardship. It is written for museum-shop discovery, classroom prompts, family learning, and future rights-cleared product testing.

## Key assets

- Galapagos Islands
- Polar Bear

## Key facts

- Endangered ecosystems and species are connected by habitat pressure, climate change, and human decisions.
- Flagship subjects help learners understand wider ecological networks.
- Production requires rights-cleared nature imagery and conservation-sensitive copy.
