## Eligibility & rights (gate)

A candidate becomes a **sellable SKU** only after the underlying asset(s): (1) pass discovery → preservation → metadata → rights/quality, (2) have a confirmed public-domain or licensed high-resolution master (Smithsonian Open Access, Rijksmuseum, NGA, Library of Congress, Europeana, Public Domain Review), and (3) carry machine-readable rights. Per ADR-010, products are derived goods and **must not** gate access to canonical public memory.
