# Product Description - Big Cats of the World Ebook

**Working title:** Big Cats of the World  
**Format:** Digital ebook draft  
**Source dependencies:** `data/publishing/ebooks.json`, `data/publishing/top_25_showcase_assets.json`

## Short description

A source-aware Nature & Culture guide to lions, tigers, jaguars, snow leopards, habitats, and conservation storytelling.

## Long description

Big Cats of the World introduces readers to the animals people recognize instantly but often understand only as symbols. This draft ebook connects species literacy with habitat context, ethical image reading, and educational activities for families and classrooms. It is designed as a publishable prototype, not a final release: rights-cleared imagery, source captions, and expert review are required before distribution.

## Target audience

Families, educators, wildlife learners, museum members, and conservation-curious readers.

## Readiness level

Draft sample. Editorial, rights, and expert review required.
