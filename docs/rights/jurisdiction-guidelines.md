# Nature & Culture Jurisdiction Guidelines

**Status:** RC17 implementation policy  
**Architecture note:** Architecture v1.0 remains frozen; this policy does not modify governance, architecture, agents, or ADRs.

## 1. Default rule

When jurisdiction-specific rights are unclear, block production publication and allow metadata/text-only demonstration use.

## 2. United States

Check:

- public-domain status
- rights advisories
- privacy/publicity issues
- trademark concerns
- source-specific terms

## 3. United Kingdom

Check:

- copyright term
- database rights
- museum open-access terms
- moral rights attribution

## 4. European Union

Check:

- copyright term
- database rights
- cultural heritage restrictions
- attribution requirements

## 5. India

Check:

- monument/heritage photography restrictions
- source terms
- copyright
- cultural sensitivity

## 6. China

Check:

- local heritage restrictions
- image rights
- cultural property restrictions
- source terms

## 7. Multi-country and global subjects

Apply the most restrictive relevant source/license term until item-level review is complete.

## 8. Production publication rule

No production publication is allowed solely on category-level assumptions. Asset-level source URL, license evidence, attribution, and jurisdiction review are required.
