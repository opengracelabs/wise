## H1: Cities of the Silk Road

### Hero

The caravan cities where East met West. For a thousand years, goods, faiths and ideas flowed along the Silk Road — and at its great oasis cities, they pooled into some of the most beautiful architecture on earth.

### Featured in this collection

Registan, Samarkand, Po-i-Kalyan, Bukhara, Itchan Kala (Khiva), Buddhas of Bamiyan, Palmyra, and more.

### Why this collection

The Silk Road was less a road than a web, and its cities were its knots: Samarkand's turquoise domes, Bukhara's holy towers, Khiva's walls, the cliff niches of Bamiyan where Buddhism travelled west. They tell a story of exchange rather than conquest — of a Central Asia that was, for centuries, the centre of the world.

### Shop the collection

Architecture and pattern suit framed prints, a Silk Road coffee-table book, and tilework-inspired stationery and puzzles.
