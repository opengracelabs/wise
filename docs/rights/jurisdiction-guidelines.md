# RC17 Jurisdiction Guidelines

**Status:** Implementation jurisdiction guideline draft  
**Boundary:** Does not modify architecture, governance, ADRs, or the Open Grace canonical model.

## Purpose

Jurisdiction rules help identify publication risk before content assets are released.

## Guidelines

### Global default

Do not publish any asset with missing source, unknown license, or unknown rights status.

### United States

Library of Congress and public-domain candidates require item-level confirmation and source attribution.

### European Union and United Kingdom

Respect moral rights, database rights, and attribution expectations even when public-domain reproductions are available.

### Indigenous and culturally sensitive contexts

Require sensitivity review and partner clearance before publication or commercial product use.

### Wildlife and field photography

Species data does not clear image rights. Use rights-cleared images only.

### UNESCO and heritage sites

Site recognition does not grant image, trademark, or reproduction rights.
