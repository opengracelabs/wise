## H1: Wonders of Australia

### Hero

The icons of an island continent. Australia is a continent of superlatives: the oldest living culture, the largest reef, animals found nowhere else, and a monolith at its red heart.

### Featured in this collection

Sydney Opera House, Uluru-Kata Tjuta, Great Barrier Reef, Twelve Apostles, Red Kangaroo (Osphranter rufus), and more.

### Why this collection

Australia's wonders span deep time and the present day — from 65,000 years of Aboriginal rock art at Kakadu and the sacred monolith of Uluru, to the egg-laying platypus and the modern sails of the Sydney Opera House. Read together they introduce a place where the ancient and the new, the natural and the built, sit side by side.

### Shop the collection

Broad appeal across decor, travel and family lines: canvas landscape prints, a wildlife calendar, a reef/outback puzzle, and an Australia coffee-table book.
