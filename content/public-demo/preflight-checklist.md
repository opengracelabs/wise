# RC16 Public Demo Preflight Checklist

> **Public demonstration only. No payment processing enabled. No checkout. No order creation. No fulfillment. No customer data collection.**

## Required before sharing the demo

- [ ] Confirm this folder is the release source: `content/public-demo/`.
- [ ] Confirm every artifact includes the public demonstration notice.
- [ ] Confirm no artifact claims a product is available to buy.
- [ ] Confirm no artifact asks for customer name, email, address, payment details, account creation, or order information.
- [ ] Confirm no checkout, payment, order, or fulfillment route is linked.
- [ ] Confirm rights warnings are preserved.
- [ ] Confirm all source dependencies exist in the repository.
- [ ] Confirm no unsupported factual claims were added during revision.
- [ ] Confirm visual, audio, and product mockup assets are placeholders until rights-cleared.
- [ ] Confirm ADR-011 scope: no architecture/governance/agent/ADR changes.

## Artifact checks

- [ ] Ebook sample has at least three chapters.
- [ ] Audiobook sample has narration guidance and source notes.
- [ ] YouTube scripts include hook, narration, visual direction, CTA, and rights/source notes.
- [ ] Pinterest briefs include title, description, image requirements, keywords, CTA, and linked artifact.
- [ ] Product copy includes title, subtitle, descriptions, educational value, audience, product type, and rights/source notes.

## Stop conditions

Do not publish the demo if any of the following are true:

- Any payment or checkout language appears as an enabled action.
- Any artifact removes or weakens rights/source warnings.
- Any artifact depends on uncleared imagery presented as final.
- Any artifact collects or requests customer data.
- Any architecture or governance document must be changed to support the demo.
