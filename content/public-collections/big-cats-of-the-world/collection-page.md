# Big Cats of the World

> **Public collection demonstration only.** No payment processing. No checkout. No customer data collection. Only RC17-approved non-restricted assets are displayed.


## Collection summary

Big Cats of the World is the first rights-gated public collection release for Nature & Culture. This demonstration version uses only two RC17-approved, non-restricted metadata assets: Lion and Tiger.

The collection is intentionally text-first. It does not display final wildlife photography because occurrence/media/image rights still require item-level verification.

## Displayed species assets

- [Lion](species-pages/lion.md)
- [Tiger](species-pages/tiger.md)

## Educational purpose

The collection introduces species literacy, habitat context, visual caution, and responsible source attribution. It teaches visitors to ask: What species is represented? What source supports the record? What rights or attribution obligations apply before publication?
