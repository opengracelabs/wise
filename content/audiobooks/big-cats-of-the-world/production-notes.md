# Big Cats of the World - Audiobook Production Notes

**Source dependencies:** `data/publishing/audiobooks.json`, `content/audiobooks/big-cats-of-the-world/narration-script.md`

## Voice direction

Warm, clear, documentary pacing. Avoid sensational language. Emphasize species literacy and habitat context.

## Audio requirements

- Quiet studio or treated room.
- No background music unless rights-cleared.
- Pronunciation review for species names.
- Final transcript required for accessibility.

## Rights/source notes

This script is text-only. Any companion images, music, field sound, or video reuse must be separately cleared.
