## Introduction

Australia is a continent of superlatives: the oldest living culture, the largest reef, animals found nowhere else, and a monolith at its red heart. This collection gathers the natural and cultural wonders that define it.

## Educational narrative

Australia's wonders span deep time and the present day — from 65,000 years of Aboriginal rock art at Kakadu and the sacred monolith of Uluru, to the egg-laying platypus and the modern sails of the Sydney Opera House. Read together they introduce a place where the ancient and the new, the natural and the built, sit side by side.
