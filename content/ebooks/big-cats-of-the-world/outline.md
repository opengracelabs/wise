# Big Cats of the World - Ebook Outline

**Status:** RC14 first ebook draft  
**Architecture boundary:** Implementation content only; Architecture v1.0 remains frozen under ADR-011.

## Working promise

A visually rich educational ebook introducing big cats as ambassadors for biodiversity, habitat, culture, and conservation.

## Audience

- Families and middle-grade learners
- Museum shop readers
- Educators building conservation units
- Wildlife enthusiasts

## Chapter outline

1. What makes a big cat?
2. Lions and the social life of predators
3. Tigers and forests under pressure
4. Snow leopards and mountain ecosystems
5. Jaguars and rainforest corridors
6. Cheetahs and speed in open landscapes
7. Big cats in culture and public imagination
8. Conservation, coexistence, and future habitats
9. Classroom activities and visual prompts
10. Product and collection guide
