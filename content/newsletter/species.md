## Species in focus

The 'king of beasts', a universal emblem of strength across human cultures. Largest living cat and an endangered icon of Asian wilderness.

## Why they matter

Big cats are umbrella species: protect their range and a whole web of life is protected too. Their decline is a measure of ours.

## On the edge

Not all are thriving. Elusive 'ghost of the mountains' of the high Himalaya. Critically endangered browser, a flagship of anti-poaching efforts.
